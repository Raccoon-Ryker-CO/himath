Low Compatibility Mode

So, Have you seen this?

Well, This is Low Compatibility Mode (Also called LoCompatMode).
LoCompatMode is a bug-fix for Himath running on Chromebooks. It turns on automatically to reduce lag.
It also Removes certain features that say it causes lag. The features that are removed are: House, Backyard, Pet and Mini-Games.
So, It is like Himath but with School-Mode turned on automatically.
