About the game

Himath is a math-point collector game that can be played on Mobile Devices and PC's.
It makes kids love to practice math because it gives you points for answering a math question.
There is 2 gamemodes, School and Home. School is just practicing math but house is the joy!
You have a house and an avatar to customize! You have a backyard and you can play mini-games that are extra-fun!
Plus, We are asking for ideas to the game so we can add them or reject them. If you are intrested, you can play Himath for FREE!
